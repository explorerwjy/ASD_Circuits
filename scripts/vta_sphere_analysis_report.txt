
======================================================================
VTA 10mm-DIAMETER SPHERE ANALYSIS
Power Atlas Definition
======================================================================

Center (MNI coordinates): [  0 -15 -15]
Sphere diameter: 10mm (radius: 5mm)
Resolution: 2mm MNI template

======================================================================
KEY FINDINGS
======================================================================

The 10mm-diameter sphere centered at VTA coordinates encompasses
multiple midbrain structures due to:

1. Small anatomical size of VTA (~3-4mm in diameter)
2. Immediate proximity to substantia nigra
3. Standard fMRI spatial resolution (typically 2-3mm voxels)
4. Limited anatomical specificity in functional atlases

IMPLICATIONS:
- VTA fMRI signal likely includes contributions from adjacent
  midbrain dopaminergic structures (substantia nigra)
- This is a known limitation of fMRI studies targeting small
  brainstem nuclei
- The Power atlas's functional definition may prioritize
  network connectivity over anatomical precision

REFERENCE
======================================================================
Power et al. (2011). Functional network organization of the
human brain. Neuron, 72(4), 665-678.
